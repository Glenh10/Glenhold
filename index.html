Why Glenhold

You're a great contractor. You know how to build. But at some point, growth stops being about what happens on the job site — it starts being about what happens in your books.

Contractors who can't get bonded, can't secure a line of credit, or can't tell you which jobs are actually profitable all have something in common: their finances are a mess. Lenders want clean financials. Bonding companies want WIP schedules. Growth requires knowing your margins.

Glenhold Advisory works exclusively with general contractors to build the financial foundation that makes the next level possible. Founded by a former PwC auditor with deep construction accounting experience, we bring institutional rigor to small business bookkeeping — so your numbers are always ready when opportunity shows up.
